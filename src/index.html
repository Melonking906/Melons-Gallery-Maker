Users Guide:

To your left you will notice the SOURCE picker! Your source folder can be any folder that contains one or more sub-folders of images. Each sub-folder will be converted into a photo album in your gallery!

When you pick a source, this program will create some template files 4u! You can modify the templates to change how your gallery will look!

Source Tips and Notes:

  • Your source images will never be modified!
  • Albums are named after their source folders!
  • Empty albums wont be included in the gallery!
  • You can included a .css file in an albums source folder to give the album a unique style!
  • You can include a .txt file in an albums source folder to give the album a unique description!
  • If you mess up a template, just delete it and the program will offer to remake it!

Your OUTPUT folder is where your new HTML gallery will be exported to! Every time you click the MAKE button; your gallery will be regenerated!

Output Tips and Notes:

  • You can re-make as often as you like with new templates, images, albums and settings!
  • GIF images wont be resized (it breaks animations) !!!
  • Images will only added/regenerated when necessary!
  • HTML and CSS files are always regenerated!
  • Deleted source images will be excluded from the HTML, but old export files wont be deleted!

When you are ready you can upload your new gallery to your homepage. If you use Neocities, you can use the Neocities Uploader to automatically upload your gallery to your neocities site!

Try it out :^] I hope you enjoy making galleries and sharing them!
